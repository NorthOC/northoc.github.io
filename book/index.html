Common Questions



1

Does your book teach manipulation?

No, because manipulation is not a solid foundation or strategy for long-term relationships.

2

Can girls read The Love Game?

Yes! Girls who finish reading this book, usually end up recommending it to their boyfriends.

3

Why do we even need this?

Men's love issues are as real, as women's, however, the mainstream seems to place more importance on the latter. The Love Game, aims to challenge this status quo and help men with relationship and intimacy issues, because being deprived of both is damaging and similar to smoking 10 cigarettes daily. Only after the issue of love is solved, a man can shift his focus on other, higher desires in life, and become happy.

4

As a single mom, when should I give this book to my son?

The recommended age is between 14 and 18, depending not on age, but maturity.

5

Is The Love Game easy to read?

About a 100 pages, written in simple language so that every concept is easy to digest. Reading it slowly will take you at most two hours.
