1Theory (10-44 p.)

The theory section explains the dating market from the point of evolutionary psychology. From mate selection to power dynamics, this section will teach you the rules of the game, how to avoid certain pitfalls and why nice guys hardly ever get laid. By finishing this section, you will understand the playing field like never before.

2Tactics (45-58 p.)

The tactics section is all about you and what you can do to improve your chances on the playing field. Discipline, confidence, overcoming genetic disadvantages, style, fitness, diet, making money short-term and long-term - you will be provided with practical steps which you can take. By finishing this section, you will have a long term blueprint for improving yourself.

3Practice (59-106 p.)

The strategy section is about women. How to talk to women, how to text them, how to date, how to lead, how to read good and bad signals, how to take her home, how to have sex on the first date and how to get a high-quality, attractive girlfriend. By finishing this section, you will have all the instructions on how to get laid like a warlord and have healthy long-term relationships.
