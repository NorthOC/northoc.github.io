Still standing

Working towards my goal. It's a grind. And I did not anticipate the sacrifices I would have to make, or how I would change in the process.

When looking at someone else, from the side, we only care about the finish line. The journey is only important after the fact. And don't worry, you will hear about my journey, once I'm there and everyone will be asking "but how did you do it?". What I have not shared with you, is how many things I've tried, how many times I've failed, and how I've narrowed the path to my success.

I'll leave you with this small piece of advice I learned. Make of it what you will:

Success is guaranteed, but speed is not.
